Features

Unlock Seamless and Secure Patient Management with Our Advanced Features

Our platform offers centralized records for seamless access to patient information, enhanced by robust role-based access control to ensure data privacy and security. Admins have full access, Staff 1 can view and add medical records, while Staff 2 can view and add patient details. Secure data handling with encryption safeguards sensitive information, all within a user-friendly interface designed for effortless navigation and data management.
